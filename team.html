Satellite Team

The Satellite Team is divided into 6 Subsystems

Attitude Determination and Control Subsystem

Manages orientation and stabilization of the CubeSat in space
Electrical Power System
Provides and regulates power supply to all subsystems onboard the CubeSat.
Structures and Thermal Subsystem
Ensures structural integrity and manages thermal conditions of the CubeSat.
Ground Control and Satellite Communication Subsystem
Facilitates communication between the CubeSat and ground stations for control and data transfer.
On-Board Data Handling Subsystem
Controls and processes data acquired by the CubeSat's sensors and instruments.
Payload Subsystem
Accommodates and operates the primary scientific or experimental instruments carried by the CubeSat mission.

Rocketry Team

The Rocketry team is divided into 4 Subsystems

Aerodynamics and Structures Subsystem

Designs and maintains the rocket's outer shell and internal structure to ensure stability and performance during flight.
Avionics Subsystem
Manages onboard electronics for tasks such as telemetry, tracking, and deployment of recovery systems.
Propulsion Subsystem
Provides the necessary thrust to propel the rocket into the desired trajectory.
Research and Recovery Subsystem
Conducts experiments during flight and ensures safe recovery of the rocket and any payloads after launch.
